Handbooks of Sacred Texts

Printing pocket-sized sacred texts for everyday carry, from the Book of Psalms to the mysteries of Greek religion.

Orphika

Handbook of the surviving source texts of the Orphic mysteries of Ancient Greece. Contains the Orphic Hymns translated by Thomas Taylor, and a number of grave tablets, including the Petelia Tablet, Thurii Tablets, and Hipponion Tablet, describing the afterlife of the Greek religion.

View Item

Life of Saint Boniface

Contains the Life of Saint Boniface by the priest Willibald, called Vita Sancti Boniface Martyris, Germanorum Apostoli in manuscripts, and a collection of his correspondences during his life, including with the Pope and other bishops in the Middle Ages.

View Item

Book of Psalms

The Book of Psalms in modern English, formatted plainly as our ancestors would have read it in the Late Medieval Age. Monks would recite aloud or sing all 150 psalms every day.

View Item

Homerika

The Homeric Hymns describing the fundamental myths of the Greek gods, along with a selection of hymns and rituals which appear in the Iliad and Odyssey.
